About St. Mary’s FC

Welcome to St. Mary’s FC, the heart of football in Glengormley! Founded in 1996, our club is built on a strong tradition of sportsmanship, teamwork, and passion for the beautiful game.

At St. Mary’s FC, we pride ourselves on being more than just a football club. We’re a community where players of all ages and abilities come together to develop their skills, forge friendships, and share unforgettable moments both on and off the pitch.

Our club competes in the South Belfast Youth League (SBYL), and we’re dedicated to nurturing talent, from our youth academy to our senior teams. Whether you’re a seasoned player, a budding star, or a loyal supporter, St. Mary’s FC has a place for everyone.

We’re also deeply committed to giving back to the Glengormley community, hosting events, outreach programs, and initiatives to bring people together and inspire the next generation.

Join us on our journey as we continue to make history, one match at a time. Together, let’s share the spirit of football and show the world the pride of St. Mary’s FC!
